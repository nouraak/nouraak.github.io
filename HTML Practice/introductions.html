About the Artist

  • Name: Noura Abdelkader
  • Age when this was painted: 10 years old
  • Inspiration: I was a very creative and immaginative child. However I didn't want to do anything productive or anything school related and therefore found a passion in art, since it was a way for me to avoid homework. I drew this painting after I screamed and screamed because my parents wouldn't let me go to my friends house. Yes, that is me in the picture and the dark figures in the background are my parents.
